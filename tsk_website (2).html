Organisation Overview

Thana Serndha Kootam (TSK) is an officially recognised political party operating within Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Coimbatore. Authorised by the Government of Amrita since September 2025, TSK stands committed to student welfare, democratic values, and responsible governance on campus.

Our Motto

"Movviral Marupakkam Naamamm"

TSK upholds mutual respect, humility, and adherence to the party hierarchy. We prioritise the collective progress of all members and the student community at large.

Core Values

  • Mutual respect and dignity among all members
  • Humility and service-oriented leadership
  • Adherence to party hierarchy and discipline
  • Collective progress over individual interest
  • Democratic decision-making by leadership committee
